A personal shopping guide

The Danish Diabetes Association offers counselling and courses to diabetics and is one of the largest Associations of Patients in Denmark. Their free purchase card helps diabetics and their relatives remember the recommended amounts of sugar, fat and fibre in certain products when they go grocery shopping.



CHALLENGE

The card is in paper form so over time the print on it fades, making it unreadable. The card also does not include all types of product groups or general information/tips that the user might be interested in.



SOLUTION

A digitalized shopping card in the form of a web app that makes it possible for the user to personalize.

MY ROLES

Content Production, UI Design



LOCATION

Gistrup, Denmark
